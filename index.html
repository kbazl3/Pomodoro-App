The Pomodoro Technique

  1. Choose a tast (just ONE task at a time)
  2. Start this timer. It will countdown from 25 minutes
  3. Work on your task until the timer rings
  4. Take a five minute break
  5. Repeat steps 1-4
  6. Watch your productivity go through the roof!
